Proxies:

Proxies were a little more complicated due to the nature of the business. Proxies are IP addressed that help you checkout multiple pairs of shoes with your credit cards to avoid the websites from knowing its a single person. If you attempted to check out shoes on your local IP most of your orders would cancel. However proxies solve the problem. The issue with proxies is when the company sells the same proxies to multiple users they get banned thus resulting in no results which means money was wasted. I had to experiment differnt proxies until I found what worked best for me. There are two types of proxies:

Residential ISP/DC
Data Restricted (GB) Unlimited

Residentail Proxies are slower in speed but less likely to be banned. Also youre able to generate thousands at a time. The downside is you have to be wary of the data you burn as you run for a drop. Ideally you want to have sufficient data or you run the risk of losing checkouts in the middle of the drop.

ISP/DC proxies are the alternative which are faster than residential proxies and are more likely to get banned if you are not careful with your monitoring and check out delays. However these can be ran 24/7 if you run them on proper delays. The down side is theyre typically more pricy and come in smaller bundles of 25,50,75,100 etc.
